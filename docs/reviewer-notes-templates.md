# Reviewer Notes Templates · V1 Submission

> **Status**: ACTIVE · 2026-05-31
> **Use**: Paste the relevant template into ASC App Information → Review Notes
> when submitting each V1 app. Customize the bracketed sections per the actual
> screenshots and final ASO copy at submission time.

## Why these templates exist

The hexastral-app rejection (ADR-0014) cited 4.3(b) "abundance of similar apps."
Re-reading the Apple language: *"reconsider the app concept and submit a new app
that provides a unique experience not already found on the App Store."*

The bar is **conceptual uniqueness**, not feature prohibition. These reviewer
notes pre-empt the rejection by demonstrating, in writing, that each app
satisfies the 6 conceptual uniqueness axes (per ADR-0015 + anti-spam-positioning.md).

Each template:
1. Greets reviewer professionally
2. States the app's category position succinctly
3. Maps app features to the 6 uniqueness axes (with specific screenshot refs)
4. States what the app is NOT (anti-spam doctrine)
5. Cites approved-precedent apps (Co-Star, The Pattern, Truity 16Types, etc.)
6. References classical scholarship that grounds the educational positioning
7. Provides demo account credentials if needed
