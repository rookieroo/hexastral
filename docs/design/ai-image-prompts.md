# HexAstral · AI 生图提示词总表（水墨品牌资产）

> **怎么用**：每条都是**独立提示词,一次出一张**。复制 **§1 HOUSE STYLE** + 某个 **§2 SUBJECT** 拼成完整 prompt。
> **工具**：推荐 **Nano (Gemini)** 出整套——系列一致性最好、便宜快;ChatGPT 备用补单张。
> **⚠️ 商用**：纯 AI 图在美国版权弱(不可登记),靠**商标 + 人工再创作**护;模型上 SDXL / Flux-**schnell** / Adobe Firefly 可商用,**Flux-dev 非商用**,消费版无 IP 赔付。正式上架前过法务。
> **设计目标**：每张出的是 **App Logo / 品牌标记**,不是插画。要求：强剪影、缩到 32px 仍可辨、一眼记住。

---

## 0. 通用规则（每张都遵守）
- **地子**：暖宣纸 *warm aged rice paper*(深绢 *deep indigo silk* 变体可选)。
- **构图**：主体居中**填满**中央区域,四周留纸边,**不出血、不进四角**。
- **极简**：每张只有 **一个主体**,不加配角/背景元素。能一笔概括的不用三笔。
- **不画印**：prompt 末尾加 `NO seal stamp`。
- **一致性**：八张共用同一 HOUSE STYLE,逐张出,挑笔触/墨韵最接近的一批。

## 1. HOUSE STYLE（复制到每条 SUBJECT 之前）
```
Chinese ink-wash logo mark (shuǐmò): a single bold motif in SOFT monochrome ink on
warm aged rice paper. ONE iconic shape with a strong silhouette that still reads at
small sizes, filling the central area with even paper margin (nothing touching edges
or corners). Treatment is soft and low-key (柔和): gentle tonal ink, the form's edges
dissolve into the paper with a soft watery ink halo (墨韵晕染) — NO hard outline, NO
drawn ring, NOT a harsh high-contrast graphic, minimal flying-white. Quiet, serene,
arcane; where natural, a yin-yang light/dark duality (阴阳) inside the motif.
Monochrome ink on paper, minimal. NO seal stamp, no text, no border, no watermark;
square 1:1.
```
> 深绢变体:把 `warm aged rice paper` → `deep indigo silk, luminous ink`。

## 5. 命・相・卜 分类印章（AI 出图,不拘一格）
> **风格：三枚印各自独立,不统一字体/印形。** 参考真实手刻石印的多样性——每枚印有自己的性格。
> **用途**：Reading Report / 每日推送 / 分享卡 → 盖对应分类的那枚印。三枚印 × 日常曝光 = 品牌记忆。
> **⚠️ AI 出中文有乱码风险**——篆书本身抽象,容错比楷书高;但仍需挑选/修正。一次多出几张选最准的。

| 印 | 对应 App | 逻辑 |
|---|---|---|
| **命** | fate-app、numerology-app、cycle-app | 算命理、排盘、历法推算 |
| **相** | face-oracle-app、feng-app、yuan-app | 观人 / 观地 / 观缘 |
| **卜** | coin-cast-app、dream-oracle-app | 摇卦问事、解梦占验 |

### 三枚印各自的 prompt（各有独立风格）
```
命 (mìng) — A hand-carved Chinese stone seal stamp (篆刻) pressed onto aged
   rice paper. The single character 命 in angular Zhongshan-kingdom bronze
   inscription style (中山篆): sharp geometric strokes, distinctive elongated
   verticals. RELIEF carving (朱文) — character prints in cinnabar-red. Tall
   rectangular seal border, double-line frame, slightly chipped worn stone edges.
   Character structure: roofed 亼 top, 口 middle, kneeling 卩 bottom. Generous
   padding inside border. Natural ink bleeding and pressure variation. Flat
   archival scan on warm paper. Square 1:1, centered. NO other elements.

相 (xiàng) — A hand-carved Chinese stone seal stamp (篆刻) pressed onto aged
   rice paper. The single character 相 in flowing miao-zhuan (缪篆) style with
   interlocking curved strokes filling the seal face organically. INTAGLIO
   carving (白文) — character as negative white space cut into solid cinnabar-red
   ground. Rounded square seal shape, organically worn edges. Left: tree radical
   木 with branching top. Right: angular eye 目 with bars. Strokes connect to
   border edges in classical Han-seal manner. Natural stone texture, uneven ink
   pressure. Flat archival scan. Square 1:1, centered. NO other elements.

卜 (bǔ) — A hand-carved Chinese stone seal stamp (篆刻) pressed onto aged rice
   paper. The single character 卜 in oracle-bone inscription style (甲骨文): raw,
   primitive, ancient. Extremely minimal — one bold vertical crack-line with one
   short diagonal branch to upper right. FREEFORM seal shape — irregular natural
   stone edge, rough-hewn, NOT a clean geometric border. Cinnabar-red on warm
   paper, dry scratchy ancient texture. The character is large and dominant. Flat
   archival scan. Square 1:1, centered. NO other elements.
```

### 命相卜 · 中山篆 / 鸟虫篆（统一风格备选）

> 上面三枚是「各有性格」版(命=中山篆 / 相=缪篆 / 卜=甲骨)。若你要**三枚统一一种篆体**(更像一套系统),用下面二选一。
> **拼法**：`SEAL-FRAME` + `风格 A 或 B` + `单字结构` + `白文/朱文`。一次多出几张挑字形最准的。

**SEAL-FRAME（公共底，每条都带）**
```
A hand-carved Chinese stone seal stamp (篆刻) pressed onto aged warm rice paper,
cinnabar-red ink with natural pressure variation and slight ink bleeding, lightly
worn / chipped stone edges, flat archival scan, square 1:1, single character
centered, generous padding, NO other text or elements.
```

**风格 A · 中山篆**（中山王金文,雅/astral）— 推荐 fate-app「命」
```
...in Zhongshan-kingdom bronze inscription style (中山篆): extremely slender thin
strokes, strongly elongated vertical proportions, needle-fine tapering terminals,
geometric and aristocratic, decorative thin lines with airy spacing.
```

**风格 B · 鸟虫篆**（越王剑铭,秘/符箓）— 推荐 coin-cast / dream「卜」
```
...in bird-worm seal script (鸟虫篆): strokes stylized into bird heads, beaks and
plumes and curling worm / serpent coils, dense ornamental interwoven curves filling
the seal face, talismanic and arcane, an intricate decorative metalwork feel.
```

**单字结构（接在风格后,锁字形防乱码）**
```
命 (mìng): an inverted-V roof 亼 on top, a small mouth box 口 at lower-left, and a
   kneeling figure 卩 with a downward hooked leg at lower-right.
相 (xiàng): tree radical 木 on the LEFT (vertical trunk, branching top, splayed
   roots); eye 目 on the RIGHT (upright rectangle with two inner horizontal bars).
卜 (bǔ): one bold vertical stroke with a single short branch cracking to the upper
   right — minimal, like a heated oracle-bone crack.
```

**白文 / 朱文（接在结构后）**
- 白文(朱底白字)：`INTAGLIO — the character as white negative space cut into a solid cinnabar-red ground.` ← 深色图标 / 深色报告上推荐,对比强、缩小更稳
- 朱文(朱字白底)：`RELIEF — the red character printed on bare paper with a thin seal border.` ← 纸面 / 浅底更秀气文气

**怎么选**：要一套**雅、astral、克制**(和夜月母题最配)→ 三枚全 **中山篆**;要一套**秘、符箓、occult** → 三枚全 **鸟虫篆**。两者都比楷书容错高,但仍需挑选 / 人工修字。fate-app 夜月图标用 **中山篆「命」· 白文**最稳。

### 印章实物 → 水墨风 2D（img2img · 照参照锁形）

> 把真石章 / 真玉印照片当 **img2img / ControlNet 参照**，转成与品牌一致的 2D 水墨 / 青玉风印章（图标·UI·动画通用，可重着色、能做活、能形变）。**低 denoising 0.35–0.5 或高 ControlNet 权重锁形。**
> ⚠️ **没抠干净 / 被裁的**：prompt 必须写「补全完整体形」，否则白玉螭龙这类会缺身子、悬空、断肢。

**通用 prompt（接在参照图后）**
```
Restyle this Chinese seal (印章) into a 2D brand illustration in a hand-painted ink
aesthetic (水墨 + fine gongbi line + soft wash), with gentle dimensional shading — a
tactile carved object, MATTE, NOT a glossy photoreal studio render. KEEP the seal's
exact archaic form, proportions and carving (兽钮 beast / 螭龙 dragon / 古朴 block).
Seal stands upright: decorative knob UP, flat stamping face DOWN.
If the input is cropped, floating, or imperfectly cut out, RECONSTRUCT the COMPLETE
object — render the carved creature's FULL body and the whole seal, nothing clipped,
missing, or floating. Clean transparent background, single seal centered, even
margin, soft contact shadow under the base. Square-ish.
```
**材质变体（加末尾）**
- 石章：`warm Shoushan / Qingtian stone tones, subtle marbling, earthy patina.`
- 玉印：`translucent celadon / white jade, soft inner glow, smooth high-archaic (高古) polish.`

**8 App 配印（印不同、字只 3）**：8 个 App 各配一方**不同造型**的章(区分识别)，但盖出的**字仍是 命 / 相 / 卜 三者之一**(按本节映射)。出图：章身按上面风格化；印记沿用三枚朱印 `seals/{ming,xiang,bu}.png`。动画里**落点 = 印记位、印面 ≈ 印记大小**。

> 母品牌名章（观象 or TBD）另行独立，不与分类印混用。出法同上,换字即可。

## 7. 落地流水线

### 批量出图脚本
```bash
# 设置 API Token (一次):
export CLOUDFLARE_API_TOKEN="xxx"   # dash.cloudflare.com/profile/api-tokens
export OPENAI_API_KEY="xxx"         # 可选, 出 gpt-image-1

# 列出可用模型:
bun scripts/gen-brand-images.ts --list-models

# 全量跑 (9 CF models + gpt-image-1) x 11 subjects:
bun scripts/gen-brand-images.ts

# 只跑某个模型 / 某个主题:
bun scripts/gen-brand-images.ts --model flux-1-schnell --subject dream
bun scripts/gen-brand-images.ts --seals-only
```

输出目录: `docs/design/raw/{model-name}/{subject}.png`
对比页面: `docs/design/raw/compare.html` (hover 放大, 点击原图)

### 流程
1. 跑脚本 → 打开 compare.html 横向对比,选出最适合 Logo / 印章的模型。
2. 对最佳模型重跑,多出几张挑最好的。
3. 合成:垫进图标安全区(iOS 1024 / Android 自适应)、色调对齐、导出。
4. 内容场景:命相卜印盖进 Reading Report / 推送 / 分享卡。
5. 终稿:关键 logo 由设计师/刻字师做精修 + 商标注册。
